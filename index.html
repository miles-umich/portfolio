Natural Hazards, Risk and Resilience through Human-Centered Data and Design

Margaret Miles
PhD at the University of Michigan

I research how emerging hazard modeling and insights can be designed to better support those most at risk: through novel insurance, design and thoughtful collaboration. My work sits at the intersection of hazard modeling, design science, and data visualization.
