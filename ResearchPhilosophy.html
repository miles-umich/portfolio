Overview and Design Goal

This design work visualizes my approach to research and design, illustrating how I balance multiple perspectives and methodologies in my work.

Design Philosophy

My research sits at the intersection of multiple disciplines: human-computer interaction, disaster risk management, and design science. This visualization captures how these different areas inform and strengthen each other in my work.

Approach

Through careful visual design, I created a representation that communicates the balance between rigorous technical research and human-centered design thinking. The image serves as both a personal reflection and a communication tool for explaining my research approach to others.
